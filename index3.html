Our Food

The food you'll find at our many restaurants now is among the best in Chinese the U.S. has to offer. We prefer using ingredients sourced from local organic farms, and our chefs are masters of Hunan-style cooking - trained to hold the highest standards for food quality and consistent taste.

Dong Ting Fish

Newly caught fish from the sea, topped with diced fresh red chili that brings a bit of heat. All the taste and freshness from the ocean in one single plate.

Golden Mutton Soup

Sliced mutton braised in the soup mixed with Chinese pumpkin, carrot, pepper and pickled cabbage and needle mushroom.

Farmers's Pork

Thin cut pork belly slices stir-fried with garlic and green pepper. Seasoned with soy sauce and salted soy beans. A top rice killer.

Braised Beef Noodle

Braised beef brisket in brown sauce, with house special rice noodles at the bottom. The ultimate flavor of a rich and dreamy noodle soup.
